Preserving Heritage

Jewellery Care &
Lifetime Spa

Gold is timeless when treated with love. Every Sanaya creation comes with our Lifetime Complimentary Polish & Spa Guarantee at our Jamshedpur atelier.

⚜️

22K Pure Gold

Clean gently with lukewarm water and mild liquid soap. Avoid perfumes or direct chemical sprays. Store in your Sanaya velvet box away from friction.

👑

Polki & Kundan

Never submerge uncut diamonds or foil Kundan in water. Wipe with a dry microfiber cloth and store in an airtight compartment with moisture absorbers.

💎

Emeralds & Rubies

Precious gemstones require gentle care. Avoid steam ultrasonic cleaning. Inspect prongs every 6 months to ensure stone stability.

🌊

South Sea Pearls

Pearls love skin oils! Wear them often. Apply cosmetics and perfumes 15 minutes before putting on your pearls, and wipe with soft cloth after wearing.

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about our gold purity, shipping, customization, and WhatsApp ordering.

Is all Sanaya jewellery 100% BIS 916 Hallmarked Gold?
Yes, absolutely. Every single gold creation from Sanaya Store comes with official BIS 916 Hallmark stamping and unique HUID tracking certification, guaranteeing 100% pure gold quality.
How do I place an order or buy directly on WhatsApp?
Simply click the "Add to Bag" or "Order on WhatsApp" button on any product card. Your selection will automatically build a WhatsApp message that opens directly with our Jamshedpur studio team for instant order confirmation!
How long does shipping take to my location?
We provide free, 100% insured pan-India delivery. Orders in Jamshedpur and Jharkhand are delivered within 24-48 hours. Metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Kolkata) take 3-5 working days.
What is your 100% Lifetime Buyback Policy?
We offer a lifetime 100% gold value buyback guarantee on all our 22K/24K hallmarked jewellery based on the prevailing benchmark gold rate on the day of exchange.
Can I customize ring sizes or request custom designs?
Yes! Use our built-in Ring Size Calculator or message us on Instagram @_sanaya_jsr_ or WhatsApp to request custom metal tones, gem choices, or personalized engraving.
